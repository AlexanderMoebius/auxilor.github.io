## `/ecopets give` (Give a pet)
Permission: `ecopets.command.give`

Usage: `/ecopets give <player> <pet>`

## `/ecopets givegge` (Give a pet egg)
Permission: `ecopets.command.give`

Usage: `/ecopets giveegg <player> <pet>`

## `/pets` (Open the pets menu)
Permission: `ecopets.command.pets`
